Notes & Reconciliation

  • Source of truth. Every dollar above rolls up from the same per-account acquisition records used by the market maps. No separate forecast model.
  • Binary acquisition. An account contributes $0 in every year before its acquisition year, then its full annual TAM in every year from acquisition forward. No fractional ramp.
  • Per-market caps. Y5 SOM is capped at 50% of SAM for Charlotte and Indianapolis (where Sophi has operational data supporting it) and 30% of SAM for Phoenix, Denver, Cleveland, and Louisville. S-curve trajectory (10/30/50/70/85) scales each year to the cap.
  • Operator gates. Accounts run by national operators (Towne, LAZ, SP+, ABM, Ace, Reef, Metropolis…) cannot be acquired until Sophi has hit a credibility floor (min wins × min verticals). Some v7-forced accounts that exceed the cap become “cap-deferred” and may not acquire by Y5.
  • Relationship multiplier. Sister-property wins compound (1 → 1.5×, 2 → 2×, 3+ → 3×) once Sophi lands one property in an ownership group. Charlotte's 4 Darden anchors seed the multiplier across Darden properties portfolio-wide.
  • Conservative caveats. Not modeled in these numbers: relationship-multiplier compounding past first-tier sister properties, occupancy improvements, valet-conversion lift vs status-quo operators, brand-flag network effects, post-Y5 continued penetration. Material upside left out for defensibility.
