Scoring Methodology

01

WAS v2 Score

Every account is weighted across five dimensions: Fit (25%), Size (25%), Ownership (20%), Addressability (15%), and Adjacency (15%). Relationship lifts are added on top (max cap 5.0).

03

Cold vs Warm Markets

Cold markets have no existing SOPHI anchors — slower Y1 ramp, steeper S-curve. Warm markets have ≥3 SOPHI anchors in-market (Charlotte only, 4 anchors). Conversion curves differ by tier and state.

04

Operator Inventory

227 of 825 accounts have confirmed valet activity. Of those, 124 still have unknown operators — the single highest-leverage data gap. Named incumbents (Towne Park, LAZ, SP+) flag strong displacement difficulty vs. greenfield wins.
