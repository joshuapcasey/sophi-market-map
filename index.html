v3 Methodology

01

Binary Acquisition

Each in-SAM account has a single acquisition year (Y1–Y5) or remains unacquired. Before that year: $0. From that year forward: full annual TAM. No fractional ramp — a steakhouse generates the same revenue in month 13 as it does in month 60.

02

Operator Credibility Gates

Sophi can't displace national operators (Towne Park, LAZ, SP+, ABM, Ace, Reef, Metropolis…) without a credibility floor: min accounts already won × min verticals proven. A Towne-operated hotel doesn't unlock until Sophi has 8 wins across 3 verticals. In-house, independent, and SOPHI-already accounts have no gate.

03

Relationship Multiplier

Sister-property wins compound — Darden, Hilton-family, Marriott-family, Omni… Once Sophi lands one property in an ownership group: 1 prior win → 1.5×, 2 → 2×, 3+ → 3× WAS lift. Charlotte's 4 SOPHI anchors (all Darden) seed the multiplier on every other Darden property in the portfolio.

04

Conservative Per-Market Caps

Y5 portfolio penetration ceiling: 50% of SAM for Charlotte and Indianapolis (operational data supports it), 30% for Phoenix, Denver, Cleveland, Louisville. S-curve trajectory (10/30/50/70/85) scales each year to the cap. Material upside left out: occupancy lift, valet conversion, brand-flag network effects, post-Y5 penetration.
